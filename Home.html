Since 2022

Ocean Park PC has been building computers, seeking to bring low-cost building services to the community, where previously you would have to depend on a big-name pc builder like Ibuypower or Canadacomputers, who often won't give you what you need.

At OPPC, we're here to provide reliable PC-building services, tailored to your needs, without overcharging.

How it Works:

01

Getting in touch

You start with filling out the form on our Contact page, which is then sent to our server.

02

Part Research

We work to find components that will best suit your expected usage, from CPU to Power Supply.

03

Quoting

We email you back within 72 hours with a quoted price for our services, along with the parts we have picked out for you. At this point you can discuss any changes you would like to make. 

04

Ordering

Once we've confirmed your order and received payment, we place orders on all the parts we require to build your custom PC.

05

Building & Bench Test

The build process begins, and typically only takes a day or two once all the parts have arrived, and then do a bench test to make sure the computer works before sending it out.

06

Shipping

Your completed build is packaged and sent to your address!
