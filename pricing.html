Pricing

Due to the nature of each computer being custom-built, it's hard to tell you exactly what you're going to pay for a given product, which is why we give out Free Quotes on our Contact page.

The basic markup of our prices is as follows.
Price of parts in CAD$ + 15% for assembly + Shipping.

That's it.

OPPC will never overcharge you for what you don't want, only what you need.
